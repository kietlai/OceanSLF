The Problem

The public issue that is happening is rising sea levels. Not too many people talk about it or have too much knowledge about this topic, so I am here to explain it to you.

According to Climate.gov since 1880, global sea levels have risen by around 8.5 inches. The ocean has risen by 4 inches in the past 27 years. If nothing is done, the sea level would have risen by 12 inches by 2100. In some places, storms, flooding, and high tides have increased by 3 to 9 times. The issue of rising sea levels means coastal regions like Ocean City will be destroyed. Billions of people who live on the coast will be in danger and the billions of dollars in damages. If we keep increasing the amount of CO2 released. It will increase the sea level by two more feet if we don't slow it down. The sea level is predicted to rise 2.1 inches in the next 25 years. Global warming causes ice to melt, therefore causing sea levels to rise. Global warming is caused by CO2. Gas-powered cars release billions of tons of CO2 into the atmosphere. Power plants also release 1.4 billion tons of CO2. Cattle and animal meat production release a lot of methane and CO2 through animal production.
